Balto Ideal Customer Profile

Background

For this project, I was asked to help identify our company’s ideal customer profile. Our company, Balto, sold an AI tool for contact centers that gave agents real-time suggestions of what to say on every call.

There were three specific areas that I was asked to focus on:

  • Industry- We had customers across a variety of industries, and our executives wanted to know if we should target or avoid any of those industries in particular moving forward.
  • Tier- Our Revenue Operations Manager had developed a tentative tier system based on what he thought would be the traits of a good customer. There were 3 tiers, and my task was to validate whether these tiers did indeed line up with positive results.
  • Integration type- Our product had to integrate with the customer’s softphone system. It was easier for us to integrate with some systems than with others, so we had developed a softphone scoring system. I was asked to verify that this scoring system was correlated with positive outcomes.

Data Collection

I collected data from a variety of sources. The main one was Salesforce, but a lot of data came from Google Sheets, Pendo, or even individual contracts.

I compiled data on all of our current as well as churned customers.

Some of the most important data points to consider were the following:

  • ARR (Annual Recurring Revenue)
  • Churn rate- What percent of customers within a given industry stopped using our product
  • Sales cycle length- The number of days between when we first made contact with a potential customer and when we signed a deal
